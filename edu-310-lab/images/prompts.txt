EDU-310 Lab Hero Image Prompts
================================
Style for ALL images:
  Flat illustration cartoon, highly detailed.
  Background canvas color: #eef4ff (pale sky-blue).
  NO TEXT anywhere — no words, no letters, no numbers, no labels, no UI strings. Pure visual only.
  Palette: deep navy (#0d1a2e), sky-blue (#1a6dcc), light blue (#bfdbfe), with accent colors per lab.
  Recommended dimensions: 1200 × 400 px. Save as PNG.
